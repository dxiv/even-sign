Gloss turns a short English phrase into a deck of sign slides and sends it from your phone to Even G2 via Hub. If Gloss has matching word art you’ll get a whole‑word slide; otherwise it fingerspells with letter/number slides so you can still get the message across.

WHO IT IS FOR
People who want fast, repeatable phrases on G2—at home, out and about, or while practicing—with a phone preview that matches what the glasses show.

WHAT YOU NEED
Even G2, the Even Realities app with Hub, and this page opened inside the app. Optional: microphone for Speak (speech recognition is provided by your browser/OS and may use the network—see app permissions).

FEATURES
- Type or Speak, then Send — each Send replaces the deck on the glasses.
- Smart rendering: whole‑word slides when available, otherwise fingerspelling (A–Z / 0–9).
- Phone preview that matches the on‑glasses slide images and timing.
- Options: captions on/off, one‑slide‑per‑word (compact glossary) on/off, Animate (auto‑play) on/off.
- Phrase library: Insert chips on phone, plus an on‑glasses picker (topics A–Z → words A–Z).
- New home screen: on idle you start inside the picker (topics list), so you can jump straight to a word.
- Controls on glasses (after Send / choosing a word): Prev / Next, Replay, Clear, Phrases, Exit.
- Keyboard on hub/desktop: Ctrl+Enter to send; Alt+Left / Alt+Right steps the preview like Prev / Next.

QUICK START
1) Open Gloss from the Even app on your phone.
2) Type a short line (for example: hello thank you please) or tap Speak, then tap Send.
3) Use the phone Preview and the glasses controls to step, replay, or clear.
4) On glasses, idle shows topics; tap a topic then a word to send it. Double‑tap goes back (words → topics; deck → topics). Double‑tap again on topics exits.

IMPORTANT
Gloss is an aid only—not a certified interpreter or emergency service. Typed text stays on this page until you send.
